The Spring portfolio experienced a significant spike in AI-generated security reports in April 2026, resulting in 26 announced CVEs. The team strongly recommends all users upgrade to the latest versions released in June to address the increased volume of security vulnerabilities, while VMware Tanzu Spring offers automated patching solutions to help enterprises maintain compliance.
